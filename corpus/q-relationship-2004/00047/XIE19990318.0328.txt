ALGIERS , March 17 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The Algerian National Corporation of Oil Products announced on Wednesday it will cut down its oil production by 57,000 barrels per day as from next April .
A senior official of the firm was quoted by the Algerian News Agency as saying the move is in response to the Hague accord signed on Friday between OPEC members and other oil producing countries to reduce oil production by 2.1 million barrels a day .
`` The decision shows Algeria 's full respect for its commitments to the OPEC , '' Director General of the company Abdul - Meguid Attar said .
The output cut will cover all of the country 's productive oilfields particularly those operating with foreign partners , he added .
The efforts `` proved to be fruitful in leading to the oil production cut by 1.7 million barrels a day for a whole year as from April , '' said Algerian Minister of Energy and Mines Youcef Yousfi on Tuesday , who is currently chief of the Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries -LRB- OPEC -RRB- .
