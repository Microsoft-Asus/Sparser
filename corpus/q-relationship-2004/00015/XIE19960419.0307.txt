ISLAMABAD , April 18 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Visiting U.S. Assistant Secretary of State for South Asian Affairs Robin Raphel held talks here today with Pakistani Interior Minister Nasirullah Babar on drugs control and other issues .
Raphel praised Pakistan 's efforts against terrorism , poppy cultivation and drug trafficking .
She lauded Pakistan 's cooperation in efforts against trans-national crimes , particularly in extradition and exchange of information .
Babar said Pakistan was going all out to eliminate the drug problem .
He said some districts in North West Frontier Province , the main poppy growing area in Pakistan , had been totally cleared of poppy cultivation and heroin manufacturing .
<ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">However , he indicated that because of a `` porous border , '' opium was coming in from Afghanistan and precursor chemicals of heroin were smuggled from other countries , which has compounded the difficulties to fight against narcotics .</ENAMEX>
Raphel arrived here Wednesday on a five-day visit to Pakistan .
She held talks with Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto on Wednesday .
