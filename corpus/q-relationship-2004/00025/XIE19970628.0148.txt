BEIJING , June 28 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Trucks , naval vessels and aircraft are all ready to take the People 's Liberation Army -LRB- PLA -RRB- troops now standing by to take over the defense of Hong Kong on July 1 .
The PLA troops to be stationed in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region -LRB- SAR -RRB- have completed all training courses according to plan .
All preparations have been carefully done , and the modernized logistic unit has already moved fuels , provisions and medicine in place .
Resplendent in specially designed uniform and in high spirits , the PLA men are awaiting orders to march .
`` We are determined , confident and able to perform satisfactorily in defense of the Hong Kong SAR , to win the trust of the compatriots there and to accomplish the commission that the motherland and the people have bestowed on us , '' an officer told Xinhua .
In an interview with Xinhua , General Liu Zhenwu , commander of the Hong Kong SAR garrison , said that the troops have completed all preparations for entering Hong Kong .
He said that as an important symbol of China 's sovereignty , the PLA troops will perform their duties strictly in line with the Basic Law and the Law Concerning Stationing Troops in the Hong Kong SAR .
Called upon to safeguard the prosperity and stability of Hong Kong , the troops will live up to the trust of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China -LRB- CPC -RRB- and the Central Military Commission -LRB- CMC -RRB- and the people 's expectations , he said .
The garrison troops were formed on January 28 , 1996 .
All the PLA troopers received intensified education on politics , law and ethics .
Every member of the troops knows all about the laws concerning the Hong Kong SAR .
Many of them have learned to speak English and Cantonese .
All the drivers have learned the local traffic rules .
The expenses of the PLA troops stationed in Hong Kong will be borned by the People 's Central Government .
The majority of the provisions for the troops will come from the inland areas .
