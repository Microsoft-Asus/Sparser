CAIRO , Egypt
-LRB- AP -RRB- --
Libyan leader Moammar Gadhafi met with Chadian President Idriss Deby , who flew into the Libyan capital in violation of U.N. sanctions , state-run television said .
Deby told reporters Saturday that he came to check on Gadhafi 's health after the Libyan leader underwent a hip operation in July .
The television report was monitored by the British Broadcasting Corporation .
The sanctions against air travel were imposed to force Libya to hand over two suspects said to have carried out the 1988 bombing of Pan Am flight 103 over Lockerbie , Scotland .
The bombing killed 270 people .
<ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">African leaders have denounced the sanctions , and the Organization of African Unity has said it would no longer abide by them</ENAMEX> .
Diplomatic ties between Libya and Chad were restored in May .
Relations were severed for more than a decade when the last of the Libyan occupation troops were pushed out of Chad in 1987 .
Deby was one of the officers who forced the Libyans out of Chad .
