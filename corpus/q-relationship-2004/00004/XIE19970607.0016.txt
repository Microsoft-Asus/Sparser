ANKARA , June 6 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">A Turkish high-ranking military officer said today that 2,252 rebels of the illegal Kurdish Workers ' Party -LRB- PKK -RRB- have been killed since the Turkish government troops launched cross-border military operations in northern Iraq on May 14 </ENAMEX>.
Addressing a press conference here , Erol Ozkasnak , secretary general of the Turkish General Staff , <ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">said that the Turkish security forces also wounded 289 PKK guerrillas in their military campaign</ENAMEX> , which is still going on in the region .
The Turkish incursion followed a request for aid by Iraq 's Kurdistan Democratic Party -LRB- KDP -RRB- leader Mesud Barzani to attack the PKK .
Ozkasnak said that 95 military personnel , including 13 officers and 21 village guards were killed and 244 others wounded in the massive military operations .
The security forces also captured a large quantity of various kinds of weapons worth at least 2.65 million U.S. dollars from the PKK camps and bases in northern Iraq , said Ozkasnak .
He pointed out that the Turkish government troops had controlled the PKK camps in the areas of Sinath , Haftanin , Hayirsiz Dag , Beyaz Dag , Haruna , Metina , Zap , Gara Dagi in the region .
Khalil Attachi , leader of the PKK , was quoted by a newspaper as saying in Lebanon Monday that his group would attack Israeli and U.S. targets in Turkish cities and tourist areas .
Attachi said that his organization would `` carry out operations against Israeli targets in Istanbul -LRB- Turkey 's largest city -RRB- , and elsewhere in Turkey and against the U.S. enemy interests . ''
