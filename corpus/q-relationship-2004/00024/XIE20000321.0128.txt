ANKARA , March 21 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The U.S. think tank East - West Institute will present Ismail Cem , the Turkish foreign minister , and George Papandreou , the Greek foreign minister , with the '' Statesman of the Year Award '' .
The awards ceremony will be held in New York on May 2 , 2000 , the Anatolia News Agency reported Tuesday .
The `` Statesman of the Year Award '' is given every year to statesmen who greatly contribute to the construction of a united and peaceful Europe .
<ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">Turkey and Greece , the neighbors and team mates in NATO , have been at odds over disputes on Cyprus and Aegean Sea , but the bilateral ties have got warmer and warmer since both sides rushed their aid to each other in last year 's devastating earthquakes</ENAMEX> .
Analysts here attributed the rapprochement to the efforts by both foreign ministers , hailing Cem and Papandereou as architects of the closer relations between the two countries .
<ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">The two foreign ministers exchanged visits to each other 's country this year , boosting bilateral cooperation in many fields but avoiding touching on the sensitive hard-core disputes at the improper time</ENAMEX> .
It was reported that Cem and Papandreou would proceed to Washington , D.C. together to meet the U.S. Secretary of State Madeleine Albright , following the awards ceremony .
