NEW DELHI , May 3 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- India Wednesday ruled out military intervention in Sri Lanka .
`` We continue to look for peaceful resolution of the conflict in Sri Lanka , '' External Affairs Minister Jaswant Singh said after a meeting of a cabinet committee .
He indicated that India will provide humanitarian assistance to the island nation whenever needed in consultation with the Sri Lankan government .
The cabinet committee met soon after Sri Lankan Foreign Minister Lakshman Kadirgamar called on Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee amid speculation that Colombo might seek Indian assistance for evacuating troops trapped in the Jaffna peninsula , the former stronghold of the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam -LRB- LITE -RRB- .
The LITE has been fighting for an independent state for the minority Tamils in the north and east of Sri Lanka .
