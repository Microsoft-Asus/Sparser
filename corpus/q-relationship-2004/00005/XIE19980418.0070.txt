COLOMBO , April 18 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The United States Saturday called on the Sri Lanka 's Tamil rebels to lay down their arms and join a peace process initiated by the Sri Lankan government to end the 15-year civil war .
Speaking at a press conference here , visiting U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations Bill Richardson said the U.S. was willing to help the peace process but parties to the conflict must take the initiative .
The U.S. has declared the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam -LRB- LTTE -RRB- as a `` terrorist organization . ''
The Ambassador said the LTTE rebels must realize that the violence will not resolve the drawn-out conflict in the country .
The LTTE rebels have been waging a war against the government for a separate Tamil state in the north and east of the country since 1983 .
Richardson who arrived in Colombo Saturday after visiting India , Pakistan and Afghanistan , will leave here tonight for Bangkok .
