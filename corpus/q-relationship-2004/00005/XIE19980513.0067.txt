COLOMBO , May 13 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Sri Lanka 's rebel Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam -LRB- LTTE -RRB- Wednesday vowed to fight to the death to defend their controlled territory , according to informed sources here .
<ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">In a statement broadcast over his clandestine Voice of Tigers radio monitored in the north of the country , LTTE leader Velupillai Prabhakaran said the LTTE members `` love their land more than their lives . '</ENAMEX>'
Prabhakaran warned that thousands of troops would be killed if the government persisted in pushing ahead with its operation aimed at recapturing a 75-km section of the Vavuniya - Jaffna Highway which runs through the rebel-held territory .
The military Tuesday said Operation Jaya Sikuru -LRB- Sure of Victory -RRB- launched a year ago would go on till next year .
Prabhakaran said the Tigers had been able to stall the military operation conducted within the jungle region in the north of the country , according to the sources .
The government troops claimed to have recaptured at least 45 km of the highway and killed over 2,500 rebels , while some 1,400 troops were killed .
Prabhakaran said the rebels had lost 1,300 cadres in the past year , and killed over 3,000 government soldiers and injured 7,000 others .
The LTTE rebels have been fighting a war against the government for a separate state in the north and east of the country since 1983 .
