ANKARA , May 16 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">Turkish F -4 fighters and Cobra helicopter gunships today again bombarded the camps of the outlawed Kurdish Workers ' Party -LRB- PKK -RRB- in northern Iraq</ENAMEX> .
The jet fighters took off from the air bases in Diyarbakir and Erhac , southeastern Malatya province , early this morning and made 30 sorties to airraid the northern Iraqi regions of Sinat , Haftanin , Hakurk , Kesan and Sarisavaklar , where the PKK rebels are based , according to the semi-official Anatolia News Agency .
Following the bombardment of the Turkish fighter planes , the Cobra helicopter gunships strongly attacked the PKK -based five areas in northern Iraq , said the agency .
With the support of artillery fire , the Turkish security forces and some 150 village guards battled the PKK militants in the Sarisavaklar region .
About 50,000-strong Turkish government troops crossed into northern Iraq Wednesday , and fighting is continuing in the places of Sinat , Sivi , Begova , Kanimazi , Batufa and Hakurk in the region .
Most of the PKK rebels are fleeing toward the Iranian border and a small group toward the Turkish - Iraqi border , the agency said .
The Turkish military operation , the largest in scale over the past few years , followed a call for aid by Iraq 's Kurdistan Democratic Party -LRB- KDP -RRB- Chairman Mesud Barzani .
<ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">Iraq has strongly protested the Turkish military campaign against the PKK</ENAMEX>. <ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">The PKK has been fighting a separatist war for autonomy in southeastern Turkey since August 1984</ENAMEX> .
Its armed wing , based in northern Iraq , has been waging hit-and-run battles against the government forces .
