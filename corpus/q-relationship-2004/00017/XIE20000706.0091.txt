HANOI , July 6 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">Vietnam has mapped out an overall plan of foreign trade with annual export and import growth rate of between 14 and 15 percent in the next five years , Vietnam 's official news agency VNA reported Thursday .</ENAMEX>
To achieve the target , the Ministry of Trade -LRB- MoT -RRB- will take drastic measures to promote exports and imports .
<ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">While strengthening relationships with existing large markets in the Asia - Pacific region , according to the plan , the MoT will also broaden business relationships with Russia , East European countries , the United States , the Middle East , Africa and Latin America .</ENAMEX>
In another move to help boost exports and prevent the trade deficit from growing , the MoT is proposing solutions to combine exports with imports through barter trade with the Philippines , Myanmar , Laos and Thailand .
The Vietnamese government plans to improve the legal framework for import-export activities while local authorities will focus on realizing trade policies and managing their implementation by businesses .
Vietnam posted an export turnover of about 6.43 billion U.S. dollars in the first six months of this year , a year-on-year increase of 26 percent .
The country hopes to increase its export turnover to 13.2 billion U.S. dollars by the end of this year .
In the first six months of this year , Vietnam imported more than 7.1 billion U.S. dollars worth of raw materials for production , nearly 33 percent more than the same period last year .
