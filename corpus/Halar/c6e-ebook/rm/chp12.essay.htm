Evolution Connection

During the mitotic cell cycle, the chromosome number doubles and then is restored to its original value by cell division. Starting with a diploid (2n) cell, the chromosome number goes 2n 4n cell division 2n. Much the same result could be achieved if cell division happened first, and then the chromosome number were doubled. That is, the chromosome number could go 2n cell division n 2n. Can you think of any advantage to the process as it actually occurs, compared with the alternative? Or do you think they would be equally good ways of organizing the cell cycle?

Science, Technology, and Society
