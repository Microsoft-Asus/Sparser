BANGKOK , January 26 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">Air forces from the United States , Thailand and Singapore started their annual air force exercise in northeastern Thailand today .</ENAMEX>
The US Information Service said in a press release issued here that the event , mainly flying training operation , is the second phase of the exercise codenamed ` Cope Tiger 96 ' .
<ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">A U.S. air force wing and a U.S. marine aircraft group as well as four squadrons from Thailand and Singapore are taking part in the joint exercise involving some 600 service men , including 300 from the United States .</ENAMEX>
The participants will conduct air-to-air and air-to-ground combat training operations at the royal Thai air force base near the city of Nakhon Ratchasima -LRB- Korat -RRB- , some 180 kilometers northeast of Bangkok .
The exercise will help sharpen air combat skills , exchange air operation tactics and promote closer relations among air forces and nations in the region , the US Information Service said .
The first phase of the exercise concluded in Singapore on October 31 last year and it was the command post exercise in which aircrews from the three countries participated in mission planning .
The multi-national exercise will last until February 12 .
