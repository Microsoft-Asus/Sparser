COLOMBO , November 22 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The Sri Lankan Army has taken steps to prevent attacks by Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam -LRB- LTTE -RRB- rebels who have reportedly stepped up their attacks on government troops in the north and east of the country , military sources here said today .
<ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">The sources stated that aware of the resurgence of the rebels in remote areas not controlled by the army , the military is on `` red alert '' in the north and east where the LTTE is commemorating the `` Heroes Week '' which will culminate on the birthday of Velupillai Prabhalaran on Tuesday .</ENAMEX>
The military are determined not to allow the LTTE to have its own way , the sources stressed .
The military in the Jaffna Peninsula , on information received from civilians , are engaged in search operations which resulted in the detection of a large cache of arms and ammunition .
The LTTE fighting for a separate state in the north and east launched attacks on military installations and civilian targets during the `` Heroes Week '' in the past years , the sources said , adding that past experience and fresh intelligence reports indicate that such places remain their targets this year .
