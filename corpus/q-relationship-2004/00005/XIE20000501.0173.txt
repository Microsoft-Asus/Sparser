COLOMBO , May 1 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Sri Lankan President Chandrika Kumaratunga Monday called for building a prosperous nation in the country .
In her May Day message to the nation , she appealed to the working people to join the government in humanity and solidarity to defeat all forces of extremism and build a prosperous nation .
`` Today the entire nation is confronted with the onerous challenge of ending the war and bringing about peace , '' she said .
Sri Lanka has long been plagued by problems between the Sinhalese and Tamil -- the major ethnic groups in the country .
The Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam -LRB- LTTE -RRB- has been fighting a war against the government for a separate Tamil state in the north and east of the country since 1983 .
About 60,000 people have reportedly been killed in the conflict .
