ATHENS , October 22 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Greece will carry out a large-scale military exercise from October 31 to November 6 in the Evros region and the Aegean .
The annual exercise , codenamed `` Parmenion ` 97 , '' will take place with the aim of training staff officers and military units in the planning and execution of joint operations in a modern warfare environment , the Greek National General Staff announced here today .
Greece conducted two military exercises , codenamed `` Philippos '' and `` Toxotis , '' in September and early October respectively to enhance the military 's combat efficiency .
