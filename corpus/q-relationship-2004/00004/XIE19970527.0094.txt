ANKARA , May 27 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">Turkish troops killed at least 1,800 rebels of the outlawed Kurdish Workers ' Party -LRB- PKK -RRB- since the massive cross-border military operation started in northern Iraq on May 14</ENAMEX> , the semi-official Anatolia News Agency reported today .
<ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">The agency said that with the support of the air bombing and artillery fire , the 50,000-strong Turkish government troops crossed the border into northern Iraq and destroyed eight large PKK camps of Haftanin , Avagoze , Sivi , Zeli , Zap , Hakurk , Kani Mazi and Metina during the large-scale military operation , which is still going on in the region .</ENAMEX>

<ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">The agency quoted Turkish military officers as saying that the PKK guerrillas , who suffered a heavy blow in the Turkish military campaign in northern Iraq , are trying to flee toward the camps in other areas</ENAMEX> .
The Turkish incursion followed a request for help by Iraq 's Kurdistan Democratic Party -LRB- KDP -RRB- leader Mesud Barzani .
The military intervention in northern Iraq has drawn strong criticism from both Western and Arab countries , which demand an early withdrawal of the Turkish government troops from the region .
<ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">However , Turkish senior army sources have said that the massive military operation would continue until the northern Iraq was swept free of the PKK militants</ENAMEX> .
