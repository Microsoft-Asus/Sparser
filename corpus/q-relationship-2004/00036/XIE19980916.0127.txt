HONG KONG , September 16 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- A Hong Kong security official said here Wednesday that the policy of requiring Taiwan residents to apply for an entry permit before entering Hong Kong has not changed after Hong Kong 's return to China .
The application procedures for Taiwan Visit Permit -LRB- TVP -RRB- have remain the same , Regina Ip , secretary for Security , said in a written reply to a Legislative Council member 's question on whether there are changes in the policies and procedures for issuing entry visas to residents of Taiwan after Hong Kong 's return to China .
She said TVP applicants have to submit their applications to the Immigration Department through the authorized offices of altogether 21 airlines in Taiwan .
Applications for renewal of TVPs may however be submitted to the Immigration Department in person , through their authorized persons in Hong Kong , or through the authorized airline offices in Taiwan , she said .
On Taiwan 's application for joining the World Trade Organization -LRB- WTO -RRB- , Ip said the government of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region -LRB- SAR -RRB- has long supported Chinese Taipei 's application for WTO membership .
She noted that the General Council of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade -LRB- GATT -RRB- reached an understanding in 1992 that China 's accession to GATT should precede that of Chinese Taipei .
The SAR government has all along abided by this understanding , she added .
