ABU DHABI , October 5 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The Gulf Cooperation Council -LRB- GCC -RRB- countries have been conducting their joint annual naval maneuver , the UAE 's Wam news agency reported today .
The exercises , dubbed `` Solidarity -5 '' started Saturday and will last for 11 days .
Wam said the operation is aimed at further strengthening military cooperation among the GCC states , but giving no details of the war games .
Informed sources said that the exercises took place off the coast of Bahrain .
GCC groups Saudi Arabia , the United Arab Emirates , Kuwait , Qatar , Bahrain and Oman .
