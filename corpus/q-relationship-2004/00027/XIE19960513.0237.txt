LONDON , May 12 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">Another case of BSE or mad cow disease has been confirmed in the Irish Republic , the 11th case this year </ENAMEX>, according to reports reaching here from Dublin .
The Irish Department of Agriculture announced on Saturday that the latest case of BSE has been discovered in a farm in Cork County .
The animal involved is a six-year old cow and the entire herd of 178 cows would be destroyed and this is the 11th case this year and brings the total number of BSE cases in the coutry 's herd since 1989 to 126 .
Under the policy of the Irish government , all the BSE -infected cattle will be automatically destroyed so as to prevent the disease from further spreading .
