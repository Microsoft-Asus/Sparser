ROME , July 9 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Italian police arrested today a total of 156 people on Mafia charges in several areas of the country 's northern and central parts .
Law enforcement sources said the arrested , including two former members of the terrorist organization the Red Brigade , were connected to the Venetian Mafia .
The Mafia , police said , has been responsible over the past 15 years for numerous murders , kidnappings and robberies , as well as weapons and drug-trafficking .
The police operation , code-named Rialto , was aided by the testimony of Venice 's former Mafia boss Felice Maniero , who was arrested in Turin in 1995 .
