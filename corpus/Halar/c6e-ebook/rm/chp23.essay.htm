Evolution Connection

Evolutionary biologist Stephen Jay Gould wrote that "History inheres in the imperfections of living organisms." Write a short paragraph elaborating on this statement.

The Process of Science

Let’s return to the wildflowers we used to illustrate the Hardy-Weinberg theorem. The frequency of R, the dominant allele for red flowers, is 0.8, and the frequency of r, the recessive allele for white flowers, is 0.2. In one starting population, the frequencies of genotypes do not conform to Hardy-Weinberg equilibrium: 60% of the plants are RR and 40% of the plants are Rr. (At this point, the population has no plants with white flowers.) Assuming that all conditions for the Hardy-Weinberg theorem are met, prove that genotypes will reach equilibrium in the next generation.
