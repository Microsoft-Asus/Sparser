BEIJING , December 23 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">No cases of mad cow disease have been reported in China</ENAMEX> , according to the Ministry of Agriculture .
The clean slate is attributed to a series of preventive measures introduced in recent years , ministry officials said .
The Ministry of Agriculture has issued two circulars prohibiting the importation of cows , cow embryos , beef and products from countries with verified cases of the mad cow disease .
China will continue to implement effective measures to prevent the mad cow disease from surfacing in any part of the country and protect the healthy development of animal husbandry , they said .
