HANOI , July 4 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Peruvian President Alberto Fujimori concluded his four-day official visit to Vietnam and left Hanoi Saturday .
During the visit , the two countries signed four agreements on cooperation in the fields of agriculture , fishery , science and technology and trade .
Fujimori met Vietnamese President Tran Duc Luong , Communist Party Leader Le Kha Phieu , Prime Minister Phan Van Khai and National Assembly Chairman Nong Duc Manh .
Leaders of the two countries reaffirmed their determination to raise bilateral relationship to a new height .
Phieu reportedly said that Vietnam will push forward its national industrialization and modernization and expand international cooperation with other countries .
