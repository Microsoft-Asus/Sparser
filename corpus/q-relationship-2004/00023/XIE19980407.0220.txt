COLOMBO , April 7 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The United States is to work out a program to prevent the use of children by the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam -LRB- LTTE -RRB- as combatants in Sri Lanka .
A senior U.S. official is expected here next month to study the issue , Foreign Minister lakshman Kadirgamar was quoted by the Daily News as saying Tuesday .
The Minister said this effort was aimed at freeing the children used in the ongoing war from the LTTE .
The U.S. Permanent Representative in the United Nations , Bill Richardson will be in Sri Lanka and in addition to formal meetings with other VIPs as well as having talks with the Tamil political parties on the issue .
Kadirgamar said that children as young as ten years of age were found killed in battle fields .
There was no doubt that they were conscripted by the LTTE to meet the shortage of fighters .
Whether it was in the North or the South the children were the main victims of the war , he added .
The LTTE has been waging a 15-year war against the Sri Lanka government for a separate state during which over 55,000 are reported to have died .
