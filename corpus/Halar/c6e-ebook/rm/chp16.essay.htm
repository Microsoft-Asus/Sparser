Evolution Connection

Many bacteria may be able to respond to environmental stress by increasing the rate at which mutations occur during cell division. How might this be accomplished, and what might be an evolutionary advantage of this ability?

Science, Technology, and Society

Cooperation and competition are both common in science. What roles did these two social behaviors play in Watson and Crick’s discovery of the double helix? How might competition between scientists accelerate progress in a scientific field? How might it slow progress?
