SEOUL , October 16 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- South Korea will contact the Democratic People 's Republic of Korea -LRB- DPRK -RRB- if Pyongyang proposes a dialogue on economic cooperation , a high-ranking official said here today .
The official was quoted by Yonhap News Agency as saying : `` Following Kim Jong -il 's formal taking over the leadership of the Worker 's Party , it is possible that some changes take place in the economic area in North Korea -LRB- DPRK -RRB- . ''
According to the South - North basic agreements signed in early 1990s by the two sides , Pyongyang and Seoul can begin economic cooperation with each other at any time , the official added .
The South Korean government has permitted some private businesses to enter the DPRK for economic cooperation , but economic cooperation between the two governments has not yet begun .
