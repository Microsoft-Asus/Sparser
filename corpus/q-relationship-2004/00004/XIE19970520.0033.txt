ANKARA , May 19 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">Defense Minister Turhan Tayan announced today that until this morning , the Turkish troops have killed 1,142 rebels of the outlawed Kurdish Workers ' Party -LRB- PKK -RRB- in the Turkish cross-border military operation in northern Iraq</ENAMEX> .

With the support of air bombing and artillery fire , the cross-border assault against the PKK this year is significantly different from the previous ones .
The Turkish military operation , the largest in scale over the past few years , followed a request for help by Iraq ' Kurdistan Democratic Party -LRB- KDP -RRB- Chairman Mesud Barzani .

The PKK rebels blocked Sunday night for several hours the southeastern Mardin - Sirnak main road by burning two oil tanker trucks , said the agency .
In a separate incident , the PKK guerrillas launched a dozen of rocket attacks against the government buildings on the town of Derik in nearby Mardin province , but caused little damage , it added .
