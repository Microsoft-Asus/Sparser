AMMAN , August 25 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- - South Korea would give Jordan two soft loans totalling 16 million U.S. dollars to support the kingdom 's economic development , the Jordan Times reported today .
The money is expected to be used for recycling waste water and establishing two sewage projects , South Korean Ambassador to Jordan Jung - Il Oh was quoted as saying .
South Korean firms carrying out businesses in Jordan would be employed in the projects , Oh added .
South Korea has moderate business exchange with Jordan ; as now its four major companies are involved in joint investment projects with Jordanian firms in the fields of housing , irrigation and industry .
Both countries are also planning to enhance cooperation in art and tourism .
