ISTANBUL , Turkey
-LRB- AP -RRB- --
A bomb blast ripped through a police bus parked near a university Friday , injuring four people , a television station reported .
There was no immediate claim of responsibility .
One passer-by injured in the explosion was in serious condition , said the private station NTV .
Police are often stationed near university campuses to respond quickly in case of student demonstrations .
There have been similar attacks on police in Istanbul recently .
The leftist Revolutionary People 's Liberation Party - Front claimed responsibility for one of them .
Outlawed Kurdish and radical Islamic groups are also active in the city .
