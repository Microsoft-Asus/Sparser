MENA quoted informed Libyan sources as saying Sunday that Secretariat of the General People 's Committee for Libyan Unity , the official Libyan authority assigned with Libyan - Arab affairs and relations , will be canceled at the upcoming session of the parliament within weeks .
Gomaa Kazzani , secretary of the committee , will attend the Arab League Council meetings of foreign ministers scheduled in Cairo Wednesday as his final job .
The Arab meeting is expected to discuss the latest developments in the West - Libya crisis over the Lockerbie issue .
The sources said the decision was made in line with Libya 's increasing assertion of its African affiliation .
Local observers believe that Libya is taking the step to protest Arab failure to have the seven-year -old United Nations sanctions against the Afro - Arab country lifted .
