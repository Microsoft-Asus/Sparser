LAGOS , March 11 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The U.N. Food and Agricultural Organization -LRB- FAO -RRB- will provide 15.2 million Naira -LRB- about 180,000 U.S. Dollars -RRB- to Nigeria for the training of high level officials in the area of seed production , according to an agreement signed Tuesday .
The agreement , signed under FAO 's Technical Cooperation Program , also includes a study tour by the Nigerian trainees to visit the seed production facilities in South Africa , said the News Agency of Nigeria .
Signing the agreement in Nigerian capital Abuja , FAO Representative Hashim A - Shami described the project as a veritable vehicle for the transfer of technology and urged Nigeria to ensure its sustenance at the termination of FAO 's assistance for continued availability of high quality seeds to farmers at all times .
Malami Nuwai , Nigerian Minister of Agriculture and Natural Resources , pledged his country 's commitment to the judicious use of the money and the success of the project .
