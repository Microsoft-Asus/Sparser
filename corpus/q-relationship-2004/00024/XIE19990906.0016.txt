ATHENS , September 5 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Greek Prime Minister Costas Simitis Sunday called on neighboring Turkey to help improve the climate of relations between the two countries .
Speaking at a press conference in the northern Greek city of Thessaloniki , Simitis said that Greece was exercising a policy of peace and cooperation , and Turkey should examine its own line , its own views , and finally make a move that would help to bring a major improvement in the climate , the semi-official Athens News Agency -LRB- ANA -RRB- reported .

Greece 's principle was friendship and cooperation among peoples</ENAMEX> and the earthquake in northwestern Turkey could not be viewed as an opportunity , because that would make a mockery of Greece 's policy , the prime minister said .
In a related development , Greece Sunday welcomed acceptance by the European Union -LRB- EU -RRB- foreign ministers of its proposal that Turkey should receive reconstruction aid after the earthquake from the EU .
The aid will reportedly be nearly 200 million euros in the form of grants and another 600 million euros in loans from the European Investments Bank .
For years , Greece , which has a strained relations with Turkey , has blocked the more than 1-billion U.S. dollars EU financial aid to Turkey .
