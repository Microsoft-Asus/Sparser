BEIJING , March 18 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Practices have shown that the People 's Liberation Army -LRB- PLA -RRB- troops stationed in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region -LRB- SAR -RRB- have implemented very well the policy of `` one country , two systems '' over the past eight months since Hong Kong 's return , a senior officer said .
The troops have conducted effective defense and other duties , setting up essential contacts with the Hong Kong SAR government , and adapted themselves to the natural and social circumstances of Hong Kong , said Xiong Ziren , political commissar of the troops .
`` The main reason behind is that we have resolutely implemented the policy of governing the army according to law , so that our troops have won good reputation from Hong Kong residents , '' Xiong said at a panel discussion of deputies to the Ninth National People 's Congress -LRB- NPC -RRB- from the PLA .
Under the new situation , he noted , governing the army in accordance with law is the guarantee for carrying out the duties of the troops .
In addition , Xiong suggested , certain laws or regulations should be enacted for the appraisal of the merits of officers and make good arrangements to displace them when they are transferred to civilian work from the army stationed in Hong Kong .
