TOKYO , October 17 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Victims of the sarin nerve gas attack on Tokyo subways in March 1995 will get compensation from the Labour Ministry , according to a Kyodo News Service report Wednesday .
This will be the first time that workers ' accident compensation insurance will be used to pay the counseling and hospital treatment costs incurred by those suffering from post traumatic stress disorders -LRB- PTSD -RRB- , said the report .
The ministy has already decided on insurance payments for 3,686 victims , nine of whom died , of the subway gas attack .
But the insurance has so far covered only the expenses for treatment of injuries and illnesses and excluded those for PTSDs and other mental disorders .
Members of the AUM Shinrikyo sect have been brought to court on charges of having staged the attack .
