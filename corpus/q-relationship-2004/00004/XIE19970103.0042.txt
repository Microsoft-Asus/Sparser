ANKARA , January 2 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- One hundred and fifteen rebels of the Kurdish Workers ' Party -LRB- PKK -RRB- were killed in military operations both in northern Iraq and southeastern Turkey , according to the semi-official Anatolia News Agency today .
<ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">The agency said that Turkish security forces had wiped out 101 PKK militants after the country 's air and ground forces attacked the camps of the rebel group in the Sinath region</ENAMEX> , over 10 kilometers deep into northern Iraq in their three-day successive cross-border military operations .
The report said that the security forces had also killed 14 PKK members in the anti- PKK maneuvers in Nusaybin town in Mardin province bordering Syria .
<ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">The Turkish security forces staged the cross-border operations Monday night in a bid to destroy the PKK military bases in northern Iraq .
The Turkish security men continuously bombarded four PKK camps in the Sinath region and about 5,000 government troops and village guards attacked the PKK militants who tried to flee from northern Iraq under the cover of Cobra helicopters .
Turkish security forces have crossed into northern Iraq for several times in a bid to wipe out the PKK guerrillas who launched cross-border raids against Turkish military and civilian targets .
The Iraqi government , which has lost control over northern Iraq , an area held by Kurds backed by allied-forces , has made protests against the Turkish operations before , accusing Turkey of violation of its sovereignty .</ENAMEX>
In 1996 , the Turkish government troops killed 13,255 rebels , wounded 244 and captured about 2,000 , according to official figures .
<ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">Over 21,000 people including PKK rebels , civilians and government troops have been killed in conflicts since August 1984 when the outlawed group started separatist activities for self-rule in southeastern Turkey </ENAMEX>.
