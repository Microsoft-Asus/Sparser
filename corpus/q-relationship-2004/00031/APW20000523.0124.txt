WASHINGTON
-LRB- AP -RRB- -- President Clinton on Tuesday received the Seeds of Peace award for his efforts to achieve peace in the Middle East .
Clinton received the award during a closed ceremony at the White House .
It was presented by participants in the Seeds of Peace program , which unites young people from Middle Eastern nations to work on conflict resolution and building friendships .
The award is given each year to a world leader who has shown `` courage and conviction '' in pursuing peace in the Middle East .
Previous winners include U.N. Secretary Kofi Annan , late Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin , Secretary of State Madeleine Albright , the late King Hussein of Jordan and his wife , Queen Noor .
