ABU DHABI , September 20 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- A<ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY"> four-day joint military exercise , which was conducted by the navies of United Arab Emirates -LRB- UAE -RRB- , United States and Great Britain , has been concluded in the UAE on Wednesday .</ENAMEX>
The maneuvers , dubbed `` Iron Fist , '' involved 17 various military vessels , most of them were from the UAE navy , the official news agency WAM said without giving more details .
According to official sources , the joint maneuvers , in which 1 , 200 personnel from the three naval forces have joined , were aimed at upgrading naval training standards and the use of new technologies and weapons of the UAE navy .
Commodore Suhail Mohammed Al Marar of the UAE navy attended and closely followed the exercises , said the report .
