HONG KONG , July 2 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin came to the Stonecutters Island Naval Base here Thursday morning to inspect the People 's Liberation Army -LRB- PLA -RRB- troops stationed in the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region -LRB- SAR -RRB- of China .
Jiang , who is also general secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and chairman of the Central Military Committee , will review a guard of honor and elements of the three services .
He will also address officers of the PLA troops .
The PLA troops took over the naval base on July 1 , 1997 when China resumed exercise of sovereignty over Hong Kong .
Jiang came to Hong Kong Tuesday afternoon to take part in celebrations of the first anniversary of Hong Kong 's return to China and the establishment of the HKSAR .
