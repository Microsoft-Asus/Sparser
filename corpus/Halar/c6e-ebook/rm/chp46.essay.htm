Evolution Connection

In animals, hermaphroditism is often found in sessile species (that is, animals that are fixed to a surface, like plants). Mobile species, such as arthropod and vertebrate species, are less often hermaphroditic. Why do you suppose this is the case?

Science, Technology, and Society
