GENEVA , June 4 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Foreign ministers of the five permanent members of the Security Council -LRB- P5 -RRB- pledged to cooperate closely in urgent efforts to prevent a nuclear and missile arms race in South Asia in a joint communique issued at the end of their one-day meeting here Thursday .
The meeting was chaired by Chinese Foreign Minister Tang Jiaxuan , the current rotating coordinator of the P5 .
Others present at the P5 meeting at the foreign minister level are Evegueni Primakov of Russia , Hubert Vedrine of France , Mrs Madeleine Albright of the United States and Robin Cook of the United Kingdom .
They met to coordinate their response to the grave situation created by the nuclear tests carried out last month by India and then by Pakistan .
In the communique , the ministers `` condemned these tests , expressed their deep concern about the danger to peace and stability in the region , and pledged to cooperate closely in urgent efforts to prevent a nuclear and missile arms race in the subcontinent , to bolster the non-proliferation regime , and to encourage reconciliation and peaceful resolution of differences between India and Pakistan '' .
The ministers urged India and Pakistan to stop all further nuclear tests and to refrain from the weaponization or deployment of nuclear weapons , from the testing or deployment of missiles capable of delivering nuclear weapons , and from any further production of fissile material for nuclear weapons .
They called on Indian and Pakistan to adhere to the Comprehensive Nuclear Test Ban Treaty -LRB- CTBT -RRB- immediately and unconditionally .
The P5 will seek firm commitments by India and Pakistan not to weaponize or deploy nuclear weapons or missiles .
They agreed that the international non-proliferation regime must remain strong and effective despite the recent nuclear tests in South Asia .
