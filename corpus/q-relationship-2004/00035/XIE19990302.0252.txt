CARACAS , March 1 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="OKAY">The Venezuelan government Monday said the U.S. economic embargo over Cuba `` is an act of political clumsiness . ''
The Venezuelan Foreign Minister Jose Vicente Rangel , explained during an interview on `` Radio Caracas '' that the `` blockade does n't lead to an improvement in the situation in Cuba , but rather , on the contrary , worsens it . ''</ENAMEX>
The minister also hopes there will be more political , social and economic reform in Cuba in order to facilitate the opening of the country to the rest of the world .
