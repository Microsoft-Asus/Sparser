SANTA FE DE BOGOTA , March 24 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- A national prosecutors ' office has been set up in Colombia to fight money laundering by South American drug cartels , reports said on Tuesday .
The office comprises 33 prosecutors , 30 judicial technicians and 39 judicial assistants , one engineer and a systems analyst .
It will receive support from the technical investigation corps of Colombia 's public prosecutor 's office .
The new office will deal with companies that administer illicit properties acquired mainly by the `` cali , '' `` medellin '' and `` norte del valle '' drug cartels .
The office is led by Alfonso Gomez Mendez , attorney general of the country .
