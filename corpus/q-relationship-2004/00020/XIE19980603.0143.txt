BEIJING , June 3 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin said that China opposes all nuclear tests and will not resume such tests itself .
Jiang made the remark when meeting with Jean Miot , president of Agence France Presse , here today .
In response to a question on whether China would resume nuclear testing , following the suit of India and Pakistan , he noted that China opposes the nuclear arms race in South Asia .
`` We oppose the nuclear tests conducted by India and have expressed serious regret over the nuclear tests carried out by Pakistan .
However , India originated the tense situation in the region , '' Jiang pointed out .
He said that even though China has nuclear weapons , it is committed to not using nuclear weapons first , and advocates the complete prohibition and thorough destruction of nuclear weapons .
`` China will not resume nuclear testing , '' he reiterated .
