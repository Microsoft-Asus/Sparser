GENEVA , November 29 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The Swiss government decided Thursday to restrict imports of sausages and other meat products from neighboring countries starting from January 1 , 1997 in fear of the mad cow discease .
Countries affected by the new restrictions include Germany , Austria , Italy and France .
The Swiss government claimed that most European Union members have not done much to control the spread of the mad cow disease , adding that some were still using internal organs to make sausages and other meat products .
Switzerland demanded that Germany , Austria and Italy give further clarifications on these matters .
It said that France would be less affected by the restrictions because France has done better in controlling the spread of mad cow disease .
Early this year , Austria and Germany banned the imports of beef from Switzerland .
Italy made similar restrictions later .
