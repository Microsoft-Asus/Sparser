HONG KONG , November 24 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- A spokesman for the Chinese People 's Liberation Army -LRB- PLA -RRB- Hong Kong Garrison said today that the soldiers who completed their term of military service were discharged from the garrison today and left Hong Kong for their hometown in inland areas , in accordance with the Military Service Law of the People 's Republic of China .

PLA troops assumed the defense duties in Hong Kong on July 1 .
