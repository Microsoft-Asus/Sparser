ATHENS , July 21 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Greek Foreign Minister George Papandreou late Wednesday condemned the harassment of Greek Transport Minister Tassos Mantelis ' plane and the violation of Athens Flight Information Region -LRB- FIR -RRB- by Turkish warplanes .
In a written statement , Papandreou said that he condemned this action , which did not aid the de-escalation of tension in the relations between Greece and Turkey , at a time when the Turkish government had officially expressed its will for an improvement of the climate in bilateral relations .
The minister had ordered Greece 's ambassador to Ankara to protest strongly to the Turkish Foreign Ministry .
Two Turkish F -16 fighter jets had reportedly infringed Athens FIR rules and harassed the aircraft carrying Mantelis back from Cyprus to Athens .
Greek government spokesman Dimitris Reppas Wednesday also criticized the Turkish planes ' actions , saying that this behavior was not only military provocation , but a classic political act with a negative message for the attempted normalization of relations between the two neighboring countries .
Relations between Greece and Turkey have been strained over decades for their disputes in the territorial waters , air space limits and the delimitation of the continental shelf in the Aegean Sea , and the Cyprus problem .
On Monday , senior officials from the foreign ministries of Greece and Turkey will meet in Ankara to discuss economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries in order to improve the strained relations .
