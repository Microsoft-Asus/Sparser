COLOMBO , September 13 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The death toll has mounted to over 149 as Sri Lankan government troops launched the latest offensive against the Tamil Tiger rebels in the north of the country , military officials said Monday .
They said troops killed more than 100 rebels of the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam -LRB- LTTE -RRB- in the offensive launched on Sunday around Pallamadhu area in the Mannar district , about 300 kilometers north of Colombo .
The offensive , codenamed `` Operation Battle Cry '' , launched after a nine-week lull in the conflict , has left 49 soldiers killed and more than 400 injured , officials said .
Earlier the officials said that at least 53 LTTE rebels and 18 soldiers were killed in battles .
The army said that so far this year it has launched a series of operations and captured more than 1,500 square kilometers of rebel-held territory .
The LTTE rebels have been fighting the Sri Lankan government for a Tamil homeland in the north and east of the country since 1983 .
More than 55,000 have been killed in the conflicts .
