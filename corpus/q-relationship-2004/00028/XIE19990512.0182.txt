BANGKOK , May 12 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- <ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">Cobra Gold ` 99 , the annual joint military exercise between Thailand and the United States , started Wednesday in the northeastern Thai province of Nakhon Ratchasima with the participation of 20,000 Thai and U.S. soldiers .</ENAMEX>
<ENAMEX TYPE="VITAL">In what U.S. Ambassador to Thailand Richard Hecklinger described as `` the largest joint exercise in Southeast Asia , '' which lasts 12 days , more than 20,000 American and Thai soldiers , sailors , airmen and marines will train and live together in locations throughout Thailand .</ENAMEX>
`` Our security partnership , which remains strong and vibrant , is one of the main pillars of the overall Thai - American relationship , '' Hecklinger said in his speech at the opening ceremony .
The annual exercise was inaugurated when the image of the United States is taking a beating in Asia after last week 's air attack on the Chinese Embassy in Belgrade by the U.S. -led North Atlantic Treaty Organization -LRB- NATO -RRB- .
Apart from the attack , Thailand has its own reason to feel bitter toward the United States after the latter 's opposition to Thailand 's candidate for the top post of the World Trade Organization .
