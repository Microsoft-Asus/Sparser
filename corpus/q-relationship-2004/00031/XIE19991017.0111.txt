Queen Noor was greeted at the airport by Cambodian Queen Norodom Monineath Sihanouk , and received a Royal Audience at the Throne Hall , Royal Palace .
The main purpose of the queen 's visit is to focus attention on the plight of landmine victims and the urgency of continuing programs to assist the landmine survivors .
During her stay , the queen is scheduled to meet with Prime Minister Hun Sen. She will also visit several rehabilitation centers , vocational training centers and the Disability Action Council , talking to government and non-governmental organization officials , and meeting with Cambodian landmine survivors .
Queen Noor was married to the late Jordan King Hussein in 1978 .
For the past years , the queen has promoted peace , international exchange and understanding and other activities .
The LSN is a non-profit humanitarian organization based in Washington .
