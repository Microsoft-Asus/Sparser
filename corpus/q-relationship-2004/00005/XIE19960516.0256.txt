COLOMBO , May 16 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- Sri Lankan Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam -LRB- LTTE -RRB- have made a bogus video film depicting government `` security forces '' assaulting Tamil `` civilians '' in the East of the country .
According to military sources , the Defense Ministry Wednesday received complaints from Tamil people in the Marapalam area of Batticaloa district in the east of the island concerning this matter .
The filming had been done on May 6 this year at Marapalam , the sources said .
They also said that a group of LTTE rebels dressed in uniforms similar to Sri Lankan Army uniforms were seen assaulting another group of rebels who had acted as civilians .
Security forces have received information that the LTTE plans to show this video to the international community in order to disgrace the Sri Lankan security forces , the sources added .
The LTTE has been fighting for a separate Tamil state in the north-east of the country since 1983 .
More than 50,000 people have been reportedly killed in the conflict .
