Addressing a news conference , Fox , who arrived here Wednesday for a five-day visit , said that while the Sri Lankan government is keen on a negotiated settlement , the Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam -LRB- LTTE -RRB- is not .
He said Britain is very keen to see a solution to the conflict as it is a heavy strain on the Sri Lankan economy .
But the LTTE was not interested in negotiations .
Fox said he is impressed with the openness of the Sri Lankan government leaders he has met and it is very clear that they are keen on a negotiated settlement .
He stressed that a peaceful solution to the current north-east conflict is the best solution .
