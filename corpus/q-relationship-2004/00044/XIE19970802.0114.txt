LAGOS , August 2 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- An American citizen has been arrested by Nigerian authority for alleged involvement in financial fraud , the Vanguard reported today .
The American , whose name was withheld , was arrested a week ago at the Lagos International Airport when he arrived with a fake Nigerian visa , said Colonel Jubril Hassan , Chairman of the Presidential Task Force on financial crimes .
The suspect could have fallen prey to some unscrupulous Nigerians who lured him to the country with a view to swindling him , the official said .
Various financial frauds nicknamed as 419 have been rampant in Nigeria in the last few years , with many foreigners fallen victims .
While fighting hard against the crime , Nigeria answered America 's accuses of not doing enough by saying that those who fallen victims always did so because of their greed .
