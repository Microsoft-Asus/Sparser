SINGAPORE , December 11 -LRB- Xinhua -RRB- -- The ASEAN and the European Free Trade Association -LRB- EFTA -RRB- today reaffirmed their commitment to the multilateral trading system .
Economic Ministers of the Association of South - East Asian Nations -LRB- ASEAN -RRB- and Trade Ministers of EFTA met today in Singapore , where the World Trade Organization -LRB- WTO -RRB- opened its five-day inaugural Ministerial Conference on Monday .
During the meeting , the first ever held at the ministerial level between the two groups , the Ministers also reaffirmed their commitment to the process of liberalization and rule-making within the WTO. The ASEAN and EFTA also agreed to establish a closer relationship between them and expressed their determination to work for the successful outcome of the WTO Ministerial Conference , a joint statement released by the groups said .
The Ministers called for greater efforts to promote trade and investment linkages between the two regions .
ASEAN and EFTA saw a significant increase in trade by 24.7 percent from 1994 to 1995 , with a total value standing at over five billion U.S. dollars in 1995 .
